Candles, Lighting & Florals

Candles are a huge part of the look — mixed types at varying heights surrounding the central floral arrangement on each dinner table. Combined with overhead string lights, the warm glow creates the dreamy evening atmosphere. Also used at the bar, lounge, cake table, and scattered throughout.

Candles & Votives

Setup Blueprint — Each Dinner Table

  • 1 central floral arrangement (from florist)
  • 3–4 glass cylinders with floating candles (varying heights)
  • 2–3 pink ribbed pillar candles
  • 2–3 green embossed glass candle holders
  • 3 gold crystal votive holders with tealights
  • 2–3 amber/champagne bud vases with single stems
  • Possibly on a sheer chiffon runner (TBD)
  • All on bare wooden round tabletop — no tablecloth

Multiply by 3 tables for total counts, plus extras for bar, cake table, and lounge.
