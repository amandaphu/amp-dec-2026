Category 2

Candles & Votives

Candles are a HUGE part of the look. Mixed candle types at varying heights surrounding the central floral arrangement on each dinner table. The warm glow of candlelight + florals is the core aesthetic. Also used at the bar, lounge, cake table, and scattered throughout.

Setup Blueprint — Each Dinner Table

  • 1 central floral arrangement (from florist)
  • 3–4 glass cylinders with floating candles (varying heights)
  • 2–3 pink ribbed pillar candles
  • 2–3 green textured pillar candles
  • 3–4 gold crystal votive holders with tealights
  • 2–3 camel/amber votives
  • Possibly on a sheer chiffon runner (TBD)
  • All on bare wooden round tabletop — no tablecloth

Multiply by 3 tables for total counts, plus extras for bar, cake table, and lounge.
